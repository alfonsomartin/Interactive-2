NO          The way that I've arranged my life, I've left no time to feel.
               This ansthetic works so well I never do reveal my feelings
               even to myself or to those close to me. By keeping busy all the time

TIME      I act like I am free, and if I have some time to spare
                I fill it up real fast, and pray this numbing sedative is somehow going to last.
                When nosey people question me I hasten to explain

TO         this is my plan to get through life by minimizing pain,
               and yet at times I lie awake obsessedby sudden doubt–
               I worry that I'm just a fool and somehow missing out.

FEEL      I do not hope to really live but merely to survive,
                but will I mourn when I'm near death not having been alive?

Tom Greening
