  • Choose an existing poem. It should be written by someone else — someone who is not yourself and someone you do not know on a personal level.

For each variation, you will focus on changing the poem's form through shifts in composition, typography, hierarchy, scale, and pacing. The poem's content must remain intact throughout all 25 variations.

  • The 25 variations are divided into 5 levels of 5 variations each. The levels start with my constraints and gradually allow more freedom.
